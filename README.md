## What Is StartupIQ?

90% of startups in India fail. Not because founders lack passion or money. Because they build the wrong thing, in the wrong market, at the wrong time.

Today if you want real market research you need to hire a consultant (50000 rupees+), buy market reports (100000 rupees+), spend weeks doing manual research, and still get generic global data that does not apply to India.

StartupIQ fixes all of that. You type one startup idea. StartupIQ analyzes the Indian market, maps your competitors, scores your opportunity, finds reasons it could fail, shows demand across Indian cities, and gives you a complete intelligence report automatically in 60 seconds.

It is AI McKinsey for every Indian founder. For free.

## The 5 Core Features

### Feature 1 - Market Opportunity Analyzer

WHAT IT DOES: You type your startup idea. The AI tells you everything about the market. How much demand exists. Is it growing or dying. How big the market is in rupees. Who exactly will pay for it. What problems customers face today. Which specific niche you should target in India.

EXAMPLE: You type AI app for restaurant management. AI returns demand score 84/100, growing 34% per year, 2400 crore market, best target is small restaurants in Tier 2 cities, top pain is no affordable software.

WHAT SHOWS ON SCREEN: Market Demand Score (big number 0-100), Trend Direction with percentage, Market Size in rupees, Best Target Audience, Top 3 Customer Pain Points, Recommended Niche, India Specific Insight.

---

### Feature 2 - Competitor Intelligence Dashboard

WHAT IT DOES: The AI finds all real competitors in India. Shows their prices. What they do well. What they do badly. The hero visual is a bubble chart where each bubble is a competitor. The empty space on the chart is your opportunity.

EXAMPLE: Competitors found are Petpooja, POSist, Dotpe. All of them are expensive and target large restaurants. Gap is no affordable solution under 500 rupees per month. Your opportunity is budget AI for small restaurants in Tier 2 cities.

WHAT SHOWS ON SCREEN: Competitor Bubble Chart (price vs features), Market Saturation Meter, Competitor cards with name/pricing/strength/weakness, Gap Analysis, Differentiation Opportunity.

---

### Feature 3 - RiskLens AI

WHAT IT DOES: Every other AI tool tells you your idea is great. RiskLens does the opposite. It finds every reason your startup will FAIL before you waste time and money building it. 5 brutal honest failure reasons. The single biggest risk. How competitors will respond. Exactly what you must fix.

EXAMPLE: Failure reason 1 is sales cycle for small restaurants is 3 to 6 months. Failure reason 2 is cash payment culture creates digital resistance. Biggest risk is customer acquisition cost will eat all margins. Fix this first by partnering with food aggregators.

WHAT SHOWS ON SCREEN: 5 Risk Cards in red and orange, Biggest Risk highlighted, Competitor Response warning, Market Timing assessment, 3 Action Items to fix before starting.

---

### Feature 4 - Opportunity Score Card

WHAT IT DOES: After all analysis is done, AI generates one beautiful score card. Overall opportunity score out of 100. Top 3 strengths. Top 3 risks. The single most important next action. Export as PDF and hand it to anyone.

EXAMPLE OUTPUT: Overall Score 81/100. Strengths are large underserved market, low competition in Tier 2, clear pain point. Risks are long sales cycle, digital resistance, competitor response. Next Action is build free trial targeting 10 restaurants in one city first.

WHAT SHOWS ON SCREEN: Circular score display, Score breakdown by category, Strengths in green, Risks in red, Export PDF button.

---

### Feature 5 - India Market Heatmap

WHAT IT DOES: A visual map of India showing which cities have the highest demand for your startup idea. Color coded. Dark color means high demand. Light color means low demand. See at a glance where to launch first.

EXAMPLE: You type cloud kitchen for office lunches. Map shows Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune glowing dark (high demand). Tier 2 cities in yellow (emerging demand). Recommendation is launch in Hyderabad first.

WHAT SHOWS ON SCREEN: Full India map with color coded cities, Demand legend, Top 3 recommended launch cities, City demand score on hover, Tier 2 opportunity summary.

## How To Run This Project

STEP 1 - Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/startupiq-ai.git

STEP 2 - Setup Backend
cd backend
pip install -r requirements.txt
Copy .env.example to .env and add your API keys
uvicorn main:app --reload

STEP 3 - Setup Frontend
cd frontend
npm install

Frontend runs at http://localhost:3000

STEP 4 - Open the app
